## What is Serato DJ Pro?

Serato DJ Pro is a professional digital DJ software platform developed by Serato Audio Research. It is one of the most widely used DJ software solutions, known for its reliability, DVS (Digital Vinyl System) support, and extensive hardware controller compatibility.

Infrastructure teams building DJ and live performance environments benefit from Serato's professional feature set and industry-standard status. System administrators appreciate the modular expansion packs (Pitch 'n Time, Flip, FX, etc.) and comprehensive hardware support.

## Installation Guide

### Prerequisites

- Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
- 4 GB RAM minimum (8 GB recommended)
- USB DJ controller or audio interface

### Option 1: Windows Installer (Recommended)

**Step 1:** Visit the official Serato website: `https://serato.com/`

**Step 2:** Click **Download** and select **Serato DJ Pro**.

**Step 3:** Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.

**Step 4:** Launch Serato DJ Pro from the Start Menu.

### Option 2: macOS

Download the macOS installer from the Serato website.

## Basic Usage

### Loading a Track

**Step 1:** Click the **Browse** tab.

**Step 2:** Navigate to your music library.

**Step 3:** Drag a track to a deck or double-click to load.

### Beatmatching

**Step 1:** Load two tracks on decks A and B.

**Step 2:** Click the **Sync** button to auto-beatmatch.

**Step 3:** Alternatively, adjust the pitch slider manually.

### Using Effects

**Step 1:** Click the **FX** tab.

**Step 2:** Choose an effect (Echo, Reverb, Delay, etc.).

**Step 3:** Adjust the effect parameters.

### Recording a Mix

**Step 1:** Click the **Rec** button.

**Step 2:** Click **Start Recording**.

**Step 3:** Record your mix.

**Step 4:** Click **Stop Recording** when finished.
